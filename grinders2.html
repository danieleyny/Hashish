Some common questions
we are often asked

How do I make a purchase?

To place an order you can add the products that you are interested in proceed with purchasing them. You may also come into our wholesale in person to see our available products and place orders.

Do I need a store license in order to purchase?

You do not need a license in order to purchase products at a quantity under one full box. If you would like to purchase more then one box of a product then you must provide your wholesale license.

What happens after I order online?

If you ordered as a retail customer, the product that you ordered will be shipped to the address that you have entered. If you are a deli, we will deliver your order to the address that you have provided at checkout within 24-72 hours.

How long does it take to arrive?

It will take 24-72 hours before your order will be delivered to your store. If you have placed an order online then it will be shipped within 24-48 hours and then arrive in 2-5 days depending on your location.

Are all of the products on your website available?

Yes, all of the products on our website are available. The website will be updated on a daily basis. However if you have purchased a product and we are out of stock on it, then we will message you and offer a replacement or a refund.

Is there a minimum order amount?

No, there is no minimum order amount.
